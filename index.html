Extract

COIR PITH or COCO PEAT BLOCKS

Description

Coco peat is being used as a soil-less growing medium in agri-horticultural industry. It is also an excellent soil conditioner, revitalizing plants and inducing uniformity in growth by enhancing water retention and microbial activity. Coir pith contains high quality of nutrients that keep the soil healthy in a natural way.

It is completely natural and bio-degradable.

We supply high quality coco peat blocks in bulk quantities.

Product information:
  • Available as Coir pith block, Coir grow bags, coir sheet and coco pot
  • Coco peat is aged and composted for over 18 months and thoroughly washed. This process reduces the salt, phenol and tannin. This pith is then processed to remove fine particles and long fibers to produce premium quality Coir pith.

Coir Pith Block - Normal Size

Coir Pith Block - Brick Size

Additional information:
  • Coir pith block is specially designed for commercial nurseries and greenhouses and as a replacement for peat moss

    • Free from weeds, disease bearing germs & harmful chemicals
    • Increases the water holding capacity of the potting mix even as it increases the porosity of the soil thereby ensuring that the plant does not suffer from overwatering or under watering.
    • Contains natural trichoderma, which acts as a bioagent against harmful pathogens. It provides a great environment for beneficial fungi and bacteria to grow.
    • Odorless and pleasant to handle, natural and biodegradable.
  • Coir grow bags are manufactured by blending coir pith with short coir fibre, which is then compressed and packed as ‘Grow bags’.
    • Easy to transport, handle and store

    • There is no need for transplanting the plant saplings.

  • Tender and mature coconuts can be supplied

In recent years, coconut water has become very popular as a natural energy or sports drink having low levels of fat, carbohydrates and calories, and significant electrolyte content.

Semi Husked Coconuts:

These are fully mature coconuts but while de-husking, the husk is left intact over the "eyes" of the coconut. This is a sensitive area, and by protecting it we can increase the shelf life of the coconut as the area susceptible to decay is protected. Semi husked coconuts are mature coconuts with distinct flavor and fragrance. They are used in sweets and savories.

Fully Husked Coconuts:

In the Fully matured coconuts, the husk is removed completely. The shelf life of this variety is little less than the semi husked ones, but since there is no husk, handling these coconuts can be done without a mess. These coconuts have to be stored in a cool and dry place.

Moringa Seeds

Description

The products from Moringa trees have been gaining popularity in many countries due to their innumerable health benefits and superfood nature.

The seeds of the moringa plant have been used in Ayurveda medical practice for centuries to treat a variety of ailments and to improve overall health in patients

We supply superior quality Moringa Seeds in bulk quantities for domestic and overseas markets. Our products are chemical free and purely natural.

Produce Description:

Moringa seeds are separated from the fruit pod and can be eaten raw or cooked as ‘Peas’. They are naturally rich in minerals and have high alkaline value.

Seeds can be cold pressed for moringa oil. After the extraction of oil, the seed cake is dried without adding any chemicals and ground to get seed powder. This powder, is a concentrated source of protein and is either used as health supplement or as animal feed.

Health benefits:

  • The seeds contain zinc, which plays a key role in regulating our immune system and regulating blood sugar levels.
  • They have good antioxidant properties and also contain vitamins A, C and B complex.
  • They are widely used in cosmetics especially in skin care products.
  • They are used by traditional practitioners to treat a variety of sexual dysfunctions and to improve sex drive in both men and women
  • The anti-inflammatory properties of moringa seeds can curb inflammation, reduce joint aches and improve the physical function of joints in arthritis and rheumatism.
  • Moringa seeds can strengthen our immune system, repair the mucus membrane and take optimal care of our eyes

Why Choose us?

Vbracegreen is a trading company, which procures and supplies natural and sustainable farm products in keeping with our values. We hold valid trading license in India and for export overseas.

Human kind has enjoyed the fruits of science and technology over the past century. In doing so, we seem to have walked away from thousands of years of our natural and ancestral ways of living and from using natural products.

We are now realising that technologies that were once thought to be essential for progress have steadily continued to harm the environment and human health. Plastics and non-biodegradable waste have become pervasive, indestructible destroyers of the environment and chemicals in foods have become universal silent killers.

It is now time to reconnect with our roots and shift towards using green materials available in nature, but sensibly and responsibly!

VbraceGreen therefore embarks on embracing ways and means of addressing this by sourcing and supplying natural and biodegradable utility products and chemical free consumables and herbal products.

We supply a plethora of such products (Areca leaf plates, spoons and cups, coconut leaf straw etc) as biodegradable replacements for plastics for both domestic and overseas markets.

All our bio-utility products are made from naturally discarded plant matter such as the areca palm fronds and coconut husk thereby making use of ‘waste’ to create useful and usable products.

In addition, our products are procured directly from small farming communities of the plains and mountainous regions of South India. In doing so we hope to contribute to the sustainability of their ways of life in addition to that of the land.
